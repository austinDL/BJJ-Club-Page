Step 1 (you can skip this step if you like)

FREE 30 DAY TRIAL OFFER (see eligibility)


Take our 30 Day free Trial offer.

Please note you do not have to buy anything to take the 30 Day Free Trial.

We do ask that you be serious about taking the free trial, by having the intention of signing up for a membership if you like it, by blocking out time and committing to regularly training during the trial, by opting into our email subscription for the duration of the trial (we send helpful reminders and tips but not too many), and making a weekly check in appointment to discuss how your training is going

To start taking the 30 Day Free Trial, we have to set up an appointment for your first class with us.

3 ways to set up an appointment:

  1. Call us at 416-744-6868.
  2. Email us at info@TorontoBJJ.com or use the contact form to the right.
  3. Visit us during or regular hours and make an appointment in person.


Our helpful staff will walk you thru the appointment process and get you all set up.

Day 1:

Show up at the academy for your scheduled appointment, and our helpful staff will guide you thru the orientation process and introduce you to the instructor who will teach your class.


WHAT TO WEAR AND BRING


For Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) bring photo ID, an extra t-shirt, and we will lend you the Gi (uniform).

Bring a bottle of water to stay hydrated.

If you wish to use our individually stalled showers bring a towel.

BECOME A MEMBER

Our knowledgeable staff will guide you thru the options. We accept Cash, Debit / Interac, and Credit Cards.
